# JLPT N4 Kanji Readings

## Introduction

JLPT N4 requires knowledge of approximately 300 kanji characters, building upon the N5 foundation. This level introduces more complex kanji with multiple readings and compound words essential for intermediate Japanese communication.

## Learning Tips for N4 Kanji

1. **Focus on common compounds**: N4 introduces many two-kanji compounds.
2. **Pay attention to reading changes**: Notice sound changes (e.g., さん→ざん in compounds).
3. **Group by categories**: Study related kanji together (family members, seasons, etc.).
4. **Practice transitive/intransitive pairs**: Many N4 kanji have both forms (開く/開ける).
5. **Learn kanji in sentences**: Context helps you remember meanings and usage.
6. **Review N5 kanji regularly**: Strong foundation is essential.
7. **Use real materials**: Read simple manga, children's books, or news articles.
8. **Write example sentences**: Create your own sentences using new kanji.
